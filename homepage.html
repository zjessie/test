Year Up and YUBA

Year Up is an intense one year program for underprivelaged youth to grow confidence and their tech skills. We strive to close the opportunity divide and send youth to a career, with knowledge beforehand. Year Up has many sites in major cities, such as:

  • Boston
  • Chicago
  • San Francisco
  • San Jose
